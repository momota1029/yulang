## Projection bundle tail syntax

- Added expression tail sugar:
  - `a.(x, y(arg))` lowers as `(a.x, a.y(arg))`.
  - `a.{ x: y, y: z(arg) }` lowers as `{ x: a.y, y: a.z(arg) }`.
- Parser:
  - Added `Dot` token plus `ProjectionTuple` / `ProjectionRecord` nodes.
  - Projection groups reuse existing `Paren` / `BraceGroup` body parsing.
- Lowering:
  - Projection items are relative expressions whose first identifier or qualified path becomes a synthetic selection on the receiver.
  - Non-expansive receivers are reused directly.
  - Expansive receivers are bound to an internal `#projection-receiver` local so the receiver evaluates once.
